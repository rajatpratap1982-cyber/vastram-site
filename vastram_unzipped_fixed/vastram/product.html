About this saree

This opulent crimson Banarasi silk saree is handwoven in Varanasi by master weavers using traditional pit looms. The six-yard drape features intricate gold zari motifs — temple borders, floral butis and a grand pallu with paisley patterns — making it a definitive choice for weddings, receptions and festive ceremonies. Comes with a matching brocade blouse piece (unstitched).

Weave & craft

Woven on traditional handlooms over 10–14 days, each saree carries the hallmark of Banarasi heritage — the interlocking weft technique that creates the signature zari shimmer. The gold thread used is pure metallic zari sourced from Surat, giving a lustrous, non-tarnishing finish that endures across seasons and occasions.
